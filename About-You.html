About You

Let’s explore the emotional damage that brought you to my doorstep.

You didn’t come here for answers.
You came here for a feeling... maybe nostalgia, maybe unease, maybe just the comfort of someone else being a little more unhinged than you are.

There’s a good chance you’re: 
Avoiding something important.
Procrastinating in a visually stimulating direction.
Emotionally flatlining, but with flair.
  Or just following a vibe that makes no rational sense (which, congrats, is most of life).

Maybe you're trying to find meaning in the scraps.
Maybe you're trying to feel anything that isn’t algorithmically optimized joy.
Or maybe you saw the clown and thought, “Yeah… same.”

Whatever the reason, you’re here now.

I find the idea of labeling people, well, repugnant, so that is just what I have done. You are viewing this site because you are one of the archetypes below.

About You

The Curious Wanderer

You clicked something without knowing why. You followed a link, a whisper, a digital breadcrumb into a site that doesn’t explain itself, and neither do you.
You’re not looking for meaning exactly, but you are looking for a reason not to click away.

You don’t expect answers. You’re here for a vibe.
Welcome, fellow stray.

The Mentally Spent

You’ve burned out so hard you’re just static now.
Too tired to care, too wired to sleep. You’re scrolling through life like it’s a loading screen, hoping something, anything, grabs you by the soul and yells “wake up.”

You didn’t ask for a website that feels like a haunted mirror.
But you found one.

The Ironically Detached

You came here to laugh at the weirdness.
You saw the clown. You saw the glitch. You stayed because it’s stupid, or at least that’s what you’re telling yourself.

But part of you keeps reading.
Not because it’s deep, but because it hits something just behind your smirk.
(That’s where the real stuff lives.)

The Algorithm Refugee

You’ve been to too many websites that were built to sell you things.
This one doesn’t know what it’s selling, and that feels… oddly honest.

You’re not here for content. You’re here to escape it.
And this? This is content that forgot how to monetize itself.

You’re safe now. No popups. No buy buttons. Just emotional clutter and cursed metaphors.

The Secretly Sentimental

You pretend you’re okay with it all; the chaos, the change, the numbness.
But you still remember the sound of an AIM door creaking open. You miss late-night blogs and messy hearts typed in Comic Sans.

You came here because this feels like someone meant it.
Even if they didn’t know what for.

The Archivist of Chaos

You collect moments like other people collect receipts:
No idea why, but throwing them away feels like murder.

You have folders for screenshots, memes, emotional breakthroughs, and failed attempts at reinvention. This site is another entry in your unsorted archive of “things that felt honest.”

You don’t need clarity.
You just need proof you weren’t the only one trying to feel something.
